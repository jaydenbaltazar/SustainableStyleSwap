UEJC Presents: Sustainable Style Swap

Join the United Equity & Justice Collective’s Community Outreach committee for our Earth Day Flea Market & All-Gender Clothing Swap on April 23rd from 12–2 PM at Kingsmen Park! Celebrate sustainability and community by exploring tables hosted by clubs offering free clothing. It's a great chance to promote eco-friendly fashion, support your peers, and connect with others on campus. ✨ Want a sneak peek? Check out our website to browse some of the clothing available!

Here Are Some of the Clothing Options

Come check out all the cool clothes we’ve got—there’s something for everyone! Whether you’re looking for something chill or something fresh for your next event, you might just find a hidden gem.

Mission Statement

The Sustainable Style Swap is rooted in the United Equity & Justice Collective’s mission to build a stronger, more connected community. Led by the Community Outreach & Impact Subcommittee, this event champions sustainability by encouraging clothing reuse and reducing waste—while also creating a space where clubs, students, and local groups can come together to support one another. Through this initiative, we aim to increase access to resources, foster collaboration across communities, and celebrate the collective power we have to care for both people and the planet.

How it Works

Just swing by Kingsmen Park on Wednesday, April 23rd from 12–2 PM, browse around, and grab any piece of clothing that catches your eye—it’s all free! No need to bring anything but good vibes and maybe a friend or two.
